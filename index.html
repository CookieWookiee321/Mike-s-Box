Music

I have two main musical projects. They are Mic. and Heptamoon.

Mic. is a freestyle project. Here, I explore a number of different electronic music styles.
It began as a psytrance project and slowly changed to however I was feeling at that point in time. I like to not place too many barriers. Rather, I target a style which could span many different genres.

Heptamoon is a dark progressive trance and techno project. It is a lot more fixed than the Mic. project and is updated much less frequently at the moment.
